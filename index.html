The reason I took Computer-supported cooperative work class is because I wanted to learn more about how computer systems are designed to support coordination and collaboration. Especially after covid-19, I wanted to see what new tools can be developed to support proper communication, co-ordination and sharing of information


Colaborative softwares that i use regularly
  • Zoom - I use it to colaborate with my teammates and class through video call.
  • Slack - I use it to communicate with professors and TAs for a class where i am also a TA. Different channels makes discussion chat easier based on categories
  • Github - Github has solved a major issue on working on team projects. I really like the feature of making your own branch in team projects to avoid conflicts
  • Airplay - Airplay is an awesome feature for screen sharing on big screens. I have used it many times to watch movie on TV and also to teach in Labs.
